Reward:

The Rewards For Justice Program, United States Department of State, is offering a reward of up to $5 million for information leading directly to the apprehension or conviction of Mondo.

Remarks:

Mondo is an alleged member of the terrorist organization, Al-Qaeda. He is thought to be in Germany.

Caution:

Mondo was indicted for his role and participation in the September 11, 2001, hijacking of a commercial airliner which crashed first into the Twin Towers of the World Trade Center in New York City.
